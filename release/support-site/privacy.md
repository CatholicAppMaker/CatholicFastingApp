# Catholic Fasting App Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Catholic Fasting App is designed for local-first use.

## What we collect

We do not collect personal data on our servers.

## What stays on your device

- Fasting day completion logs
- Optional Friday notes
- Intermittent fasting sessions
- Premium planning and reflection entries
- First-run onboarding and paywall progress state

## Data sharing

No automatic upload is performed. Data leaves your device only if you explicitly use an export/share action.

## Third-party analytics and tracking

- No third-party analytics SDK
- No ad-tracking identifiers
- No behavioral profiling
