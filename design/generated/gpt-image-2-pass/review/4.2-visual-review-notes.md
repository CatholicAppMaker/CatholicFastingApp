# 4.2 Visual Review Notes

## Code polish applied
- Today now leads with the actual daily fasting decision before sacred imagery.
- Fasting Days now leads with the next required day before decorative/reference imagery.
- Track Fast now leads with the live tracker before the hero image.
- Sacred hero cards are shorter and quieter on secondary surfaces.
- Quote cards were removed from secondary hero stacks where they competed with the main answer.
- The sacred gallery was trimmed to unique imagery instead of repeated variants of the same symbols.
- Shared card/glass styling was tightened: lower utility-card opacity, smaller shadows, calmer radii.
- iPad primary cards now use the primary card tier where the workspace's main lane needs emphasis.
- Premium plan buttons now reserve the prominent button treatment for the primary yearly anchor.

## Current asset review
- The current icon family is coherent and reads clearly at contact-sheet scale.
- The pre-GPT Image 2 HeroSacred, GuidanceSacred, and SacredScriptureCandle assets shared very similar candle/scripture imagery.
- The promoted GPT Image 2 set gives the app clearer art separation:
  - HeroSacred: quiet chapel altar and sanctuary lamp.
  - GuidanceSacred: practical pastoral guidance still life with bread, water, rosary, and abstract rule page.
  - SacredScriptureCandle: premium/reflection desk with prayer journal and blank planner pages.
  - Social header: deeper aubergine/gold sacred atmosphere with stronger external-brand presence.
- The icon family remains strong enough to keep for now; the biggest visual lift came from replacing repeated in-app art.

## GPT Image 2 promotion gate
Only promote new generated art if it beats the current asset in:
- small-size icon readability
- distinct seasonal identity without breaking the icon family
- reverence and restraint
- in-app readability under title overlays
- App Store screenshot background discipline
