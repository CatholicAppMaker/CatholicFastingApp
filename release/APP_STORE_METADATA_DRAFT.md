## Description
Catholic Fasting helps you plan and track fasting observances through the year using U.S.-aligned liturgical rules.

Features:
- Calendar of mandatory and optional observances
- Fast and abstinence guidance for common scenarios
- Personal tracking with statuses and notes
- Rule-bundle transparency with sources and rationale
- iCloud sync toggle and export controls
- English and Spanish support

Important:
This app supports personal discipline and pastoral formation. It does not replace medical or pastoral advice.
