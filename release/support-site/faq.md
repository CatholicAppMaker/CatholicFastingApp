# Catholic Fasting App FAQ

## Is this an official Church app?

No. It is an independent devotional app.

## What rules does it follow?

Core guidance references U.S. Catholic liturgical and fasting guidance and supports region profile messaging for U.S. and Canada.

## Do I need to enter my birthday?

No. The app uses age-eligibility toggles for fasting and abstinence guidance.

## Does the app collect my data?

No automatic data collection is performed. Data is local unless you explicitly export/share.

## What does Premium include?

Formation Toolkit pillars:

- Planning
- Accountability
- Reflection

## Are tips required?

No. Tips are optional and do not unlock features.
