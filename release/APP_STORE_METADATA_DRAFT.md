## Description
Catholic Fasting helps you understand what the Church asks today, plan ahead for fasting and abstinence days, and build a steadier rhythm of discipline through the liturgical year.

Features:
- Daily Catholic fasting and abstinence guidance with clear pastoral context
- Calendar of required and optional observances for planning ahead
- Canada national-baseline guidance plus U.S. profile support
- English, Spanish, and French Canadian onboarding and core flows
- Track Fast with live countdowns, presets, and editable start times
- Guided Seasonal Journey for Lent, Advent, and Ordinary Time in Premium
- Personal observance tracking, notes, local-only storage, and export controls
- Rule transparency with citations and source context
- Optional Premium Monthly and Premium Yearly plans
- Optional one-time tips to support the app without unlocking features

Important:
This app supports personal discipline and pastoral formation. It does not replace medical advice, pastoral advice, or local diocesan authority.
